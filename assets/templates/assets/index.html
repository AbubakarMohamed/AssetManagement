How Asset Lending Works

Asset lending at Swahilipot Hub allows individuals or departments to borrow assets for specific purposes. Here's how it works:

  • Fill out the lending form specifying the asset and quantity needed.
  • The request will be reviewed and approved based on availability and usage policies.
  • Once approved, the asset is allocated for the specified period.
  • After use, return the asset in good condition to avoid penalties.

Lending Conditions

Before borrowing assets, please consider the following conditions:

  • Assets can only be borrowed for official purposes approved by management.
  • Ensure proper care and handling of borrowed assets to avoid damage.
  • Return assets on or before the specified return date to maintain availability for others.
  • Report any issues or damages promptly to avoid penalties.
